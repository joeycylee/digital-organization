# 📱 Phone Folder Structure Template

## How This Works:
Your phone will automatically create these folders when you buy the template.
All your files will be moved to the correct folder automatically.

---

## 📂 Folder Structure:

### 📷 PHOTOS FOLDER
Contains: All your photos and videos

### 📄 FILES FOLDER
Contains: All your documents, PDFs, and files

### 📧 EMAILS FOLDER
Contains: All your email attachments and saved files

### 💰 FINANCES FOLDER
Contains: Bills, receipts, bank statements

### 👨‍👩‍👧 FAMILY FOLDER
Contains: Photos and files related to family

### 🏥 HEALTH FOLDER
Contains: Medical documents, prescriptions, insurance

### 🏠 HOME FOLDER
Contains: Home improvement files, manuals, warranties

### 🎓 SCHOOL FOLDER
Contains: School documents, certificates, reports

### ✈️ TRAVEL FOLDER
Contains: Travel documents, itineraries, booking confirmations

### 🎨 PROJECTS FOLDER
Contains: Creative projects, hobbies, work files

---

---

## 📝 Easy Rules (Follow These):

1. **All photos go to PHOTOS FOLDER**
2. **All documents go to FILES FOLDER**
3. **All bills and money stuff go to FINANCES FOLDER**
4. **All family stuff goes to FAMILY FOLDER**
5. **All health stuff goes to HEALTH FOLDER**
